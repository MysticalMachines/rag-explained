2. REMOTE WORK POLICY
================================================================================

TechVault operates as a remote-first company with the following guidelines:

ELIGIBILITY:
All full-time employees are eligible for fully remote work. Part-time employees may work remotely with manager approval.

WORK LOCATION:
- Employees may work from anywhere within their country of employment
- International remote work requires HR approval and must comply with local labor laws
- Employees must maintain a dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps)

WORKING HOURS:
- Core collaboration hours: 10 AM - 3 PM in your local timezone
- Employees must be available during core hours for meetings and team collaboration
- Flexible scheduling is encouraged outside core hours
- Total expected working hours: 40 hours per week for full-time employees

COMMUNICATION EXPECTATIONS:
- Respond to Slack messages within 2 hours during working hours
- Set status to "Away" when unavailable
- Use video for team meetings when possible
- Update your calendar with time off and focus time blocks

CO-WORKING SPACE ALLOWANCE:
- $200/month stipend for co-working space membership (optional)
- Submit receipts through Expensify for reimbursement

OFFICE VISITS:
- Quarterly team meetups are organized in major cities
- Travel and accommodation covered by the company
- Optional attendance, but highly encouraged

3. PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
================================================================================

TechVault believes in the importance of rest and rejuvenation.

PTO ALLOCATION:
- 0-2 years of service: 20 days per year
- 3-5 years of service: 25 days per year
- 6+ years of service: 30 days per year
- PTO accrues monthly and is prorated for part-year employees

UNLIMITED PTO PILOT:
Starting Q2 2025, the Engineering and Product teams will pilot an unlimited PTO policy. Performance expectations remain the same, and employees must coordinate time off with their teams.

HOLIDAYS:
In addition to PTO, TechVault observes 10 company holidays per year:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day (US) / equivalent in other countries
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (US) / equivalent in other countries
- Day after Thanksgiving
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve

SICK LEAVE:
- 10 days of sick leave per year (separate from PTO)
- Does not roll over year to year
- No doctor's note required for absences under 3 consecutive days

PARENTAL LEAVE:
- 16 weeks fully paid for primary caregivers
- 8 weeks fully paid for secondary caregivers
- Must be taken within first year of child's birth or adoption

PTO REQUEST PROCESS:
1. Submit request in BambooHR at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Manager approval required
3. Update team calendar and set Slack status
4. Delegate urgent tasks before leave

4. HEALTH & WELLNESS BENEFITS
================================================================================

HEALTH INSURANCE:
- Premium health insurance with 90% employer coverage
- Options include PPO and HDP plans
- Dental and vision included at no additional cost
- Coverage begins on your first day of employment

MENTAL HEALTH SUPPORT:
- 12 free therapy sessions per year through our EAP (Employee Assistance Program)
- Access to Headspace and Calm meditation apps (company-paid)
- Monthly mental health days encouraged (counted as sick leave)

WELLNESS STIPEND:
- $100/month for gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellness activities
- Can be used for yoga, personal training, sports equipment, etc.
- Submit receipts through Expensify

HEALTH SAVINGS ACCOUNT (HSA):
- Available for employees on HDP plans
- Company contributes $1,000 annually for individual coverage
- Company contributes $2,000 annually for family coverage

401(K) RETIREMENT PLAN:
- Company matches 100% up to 4% of salary
- Immediate vesting
- Managed through Fidelity

6. CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR:
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Communicate professionally in all channels
- Maintain confidentiality of company and customer data
- Report concerns to HR immediately

ZERO TOLERANCE POLICIES:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Disclosure of confidential information
- Insider trading
- Conflicts of interest without disclosure

SECURITY PROTOCOLS:
- Use company-provided equipment for work
- Enable 2FA on all work accounts
- Never share passwords
- Lock your computer when away from desk
- Report security incidents within 1 hour

SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY:
- You may mention working at TechVault
- Do not disclose confidential company information
- Personal opinions should be clearly marked as such
- Do not speak on behalf of the company without authorization

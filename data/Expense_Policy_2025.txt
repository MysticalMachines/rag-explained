TECHVAULT INC.
EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
Updated: January 2025

================================================================================
POLICY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This policy outlines the guidelines for business-related expenses and the reimbursement process for TechVault employees.

GUIDING PRINCIPLES:
- Spend company money as if it were your own
- Be reasonable and cost-conscious
- When in doubt, ask your manager
- Always obtain pre-approval for expenses over $500

EFFECTIVE DATE: January 1, 2025
APPLIES TO: All full-time and part-time employees

================================================================================
REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
================================================================================

TRAVEL EXPENSES:

Airfare:
- Book economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Premium economy allowed for flights over 6 hours
- Business class requires VP approval for flights over 10 hours
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Use company travel portal (TravelPerk) for best rates

Ground Transportation:
- Rideshare (Uber/Lyft): Allowed for business travel
- Rental cars: Compact or mid-size preferred, pre-approval needed
- Mileage: $0.67 per mile for personal vehicle use (2025 IRS rate)
- Parking: Reasonable parking fees at airports, hotels, client sites
- Public transit: Always reimbursable (subway, bus, train)

Lodging:
- Maximum $250/night in major cities (NYC, SF, LA, London)
- Maximum $175/night in other locations
- Use company preferred hotels when available
- Direct bill to company when possible
- Extended stays (7+ nights): Consider Airbnb with kitchen

Meals:
- Breakfast: Up to $20
- Lunch: Up to $30
- Dinner: Up to $60
- Alcohol: Maximum 2 drinks, total not to exceed $30
- Client meals: Reasonable amounts, no preset limit
- Tips: 15-20% standard

EQUIPMENT & TECHNOLOGY:

Home Office Setup:
- One-time $1,500 stipend (see Employee Handbook)
- Ergonomic chair: Up to $600
- Desk: Up to $500
- Monitor: Up to $400
- Additional equipment requires manager approval

Recurring Stipends:
- Internet: $50/month (no receipt required)
- Mobile phone: $75/month (no receipt required)
- Co-working space: $200/month (receipt required)
- Wellness: $100/month (receipt required)

Software & Subscriptions:
- Work-related software: Pre-approval required
- Licenses must be in company name when possible
- Submit monthly/annual costs separately
- Examples: Grammarly, Notion personal, ChatGPT Plus

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT:

Conferences:
- Registration fees: Fully covered (pre-approval needed)
- Travel and lodging: Follow travel expense guidelines
- Up to 2 conferences per year per employee
- Additional conferences require VP approval

Courses & Certifications:
- Annual budget: $2,000 per employee
- Online courses: Udemy, Coursera, Pluralsight, etc.
- Certification exams: AWS, Google Cloud, CISSP, etc.
- Books: Up to $50/month
- Must be relevant to role or career development

TEAM EVENTS & ENTERTAINMENT:

Team Meals:
- Team lunches: Up to $30/person
- Team dinners: Up to $75/person
- Manager approval required for groups of 6+
- Alcohol: Maximum 2 drinks per person

Client Entertainment:
- Meals: Reasonable amounts based on context
- Events: Pre-approval required for tickets over $200/person
- Gifts: Maximum $100/person, must be business-appropriate
- No cash gifts allowed

Team Building:
- Quarterly team events: Budget set by department
- Virtual events: Up to $50/person (games, activities)
- Annual company retreat: Fully covered

================================================================================
NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
================================================================================

The following expenses will NOT be reimbursed:

PERSONAL EXPENSES:
- Personal grooming (haircuts, spa, etc.)
- Gym memberships (use wellness stipend instead)
- Personal entertainment
- Traffic violations, parking tickets
- Personal travel or vacation
- Commuting to regular workplace
- Personal phone calls

LUXURY ITEMS:
- First-class airfare (without exec approval)
- Luxury car rentals (Mercedes, BMW, etc.)
- Hotel mini-bar charges
- In-room movies
- Room service (unless working late, with manager approval)
- Excessive meal costs beyond guidelines

OTHER:
- Late fees or interest charges
- Expenses without receipts (over $25)
- Expenses submitted more than 90 days after incurred
- Expenses not related to TechVault business

================================================================================
EXPENSE SUBMISSION PROCESS
================================================================================

USING EXPENSIFY:

Step 1: Capture Receipt
- Take photo of receipt immediately
- Email receipts to receipts@techvault.expensify.com
- Receipts required for expenses over $25
- Keep physical receipts until reimbursed

Step 2: Create Expense Report
- Log into Expensify (expensify.com)
- Create new report
- Add expenses from captured receipts
- Categorize each expense correctly
- Add business purpose description

Step 3: Submit for Approval
- Submit report by end of month
- Manager reviews and approves
- Finance team processes payment
- Reimbursement via direct deposit within 10 business days

REQUIRED INFORMATION:
- Date of expense
- Amount in original currency
- Vendor/merchant name
- Business purpose (be specific!)
- Category (meals, travel, equipment, etc.)
- Receipt or invoice

EXPENSE CATEGORIES:
- Airfare
- Lodging
- Ground Transportation
- Meals - Team
- Meals - Client
- Office Equipment
- Software & Subscriptions
- Professional Development
- Co-working Space
- Internet
- Mobile Phone
- Wellness
- Other (describe)

SUBMISSION DEADLINES:
- Submit within 30 days of expense date
- Monthly cutoff: Last day of each month
- Late submissions (31-60 days): Require manager explanation
- Very late submissions (61-90 days): Require VP approval
- Expenses over 90 days old: Not reimbursable

================================================================================
COMPANY CREDIT CARDS
================================================================================

ELIGIBILITY:
- Directors and above: Automatic
- Managers: Upon request with VP approval
- Individual contributors: Special cases only

CARD USAGE:
- Only for pre-approved business expenses
- Never for personal use
- Report lost/stolen cards immediately
- Review statements monthly
- Reconcile in Expensify by 10th of each month

LIMITS:
- Director: $10,000/month
- VP: $25,000/month
- Executive: $50,000/month
- Higher limits require CFO approval

CONSEQUENCES OF MISUSE:
- First offense: Written warning
- Second offense: Card revoked
- Serious violations: Termination and legal action

================================================================================
PRE-APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

WHEN TO GET PRE-APPROVAL:

Always Required:
- Any single expense over $500
- International travel
- Client entertainment over $200/person
- Conference registration
- Software purchases
- Equipment not covered by stipend

How to Get Approval:
1. Email manager with details: what, why, estimated cost
2. Include relevant links or quotes
3. Get written approval before purchase
4. Attach approval email to expense report

Emergency Situations:
- If urgent and manager unavailable, use best judgment
- Document decision rationale
- Inform manager ASAP
- Be prepared to justify expense

================================================================================
INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL & EXPENSES
================================================================================

CURRENCY:
- Pay in local currency when possible for best rates
- Expensify auto-converts to USD
- Credit card foreign transaction fees reimbursable

PER DIEM RATES:
For extended international travel, per diem option available:
- Use GSA rates for international locations
- Manager approval required
- Receipts not required for meals under per diem
- Lodging receipts still required

TRAVEL INSURANCE:
- Medical: Covered by company policy
- Trip cancellation: Included with corporate card
- Lost luggage: File claim with airline first

VISAS & DOCUMENTS:
- Business visa fees: Fully reimbursable
- Passport fees: Not reimbursable (personal document)
- Expedited processing: Reimbursable if business need

================================================================================
MANAGER RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

Managers must:
- Review and approve expense reports within 5 business days
- Ensure expenses align with this policy
- Question unusual or excessive expenses
- Approve pre-approval requests promptly
- Set good example with own expense practices

Managers have authority to:
- Approve expenses up to $2,000 without escalation
- Deny expenses not compliant with policy
- Request additional documentation
- Escalate unusual cases to Finance

================================================================================
POLICY VIOLATIONS
================================================================================

VIOLATION EXAMPLES:
- Submitting personal expenses as business expenses
- Inflating expense amounts
- Creating false receipts
- Claiming expenses never incurred
- Excessive or unreasonable spending

CONSEQUENCES:
- Minor violations: Expense denied, coaching provided
- Repeated violations: Written warning, possible suspension
- Serious violations: Termination, legal action, repayment required

REPORTING VIOLATIONS:
- Report suspected violations to hr@techvault.io
- Anonymous reporting available
- No retaliation for good-faith reports
- All reports investigated thoroughly

================================================================================
SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES
================================================================================

RELOCATION:
- Company-approved relocations have separate policy
- Contact HR for relocation package details

CONFERENCES AS SPEAKER:
- If TechVault sponsors your talk, expenses fully covered
- If personal speaking opportunity, discuss with manager
- May qualify for professional development budget

CUSTOMER VISITS:
- All reasonable expenses covered
- No preset limits for strategic customer meetings
- Keep CFO informed of large customer entertainment

NATURAL DISASTERS / EMERGENCIES:
- Safety first - spend what's needed
- Document everything
- Inform manager ASAP
- Special approval process available

6. Q&A SESSION
================================================================================

Q: "Will the unlimited PTO pilot come with any minimum required days off?"
A (Sarah): "Yes! We'll require minimum 15 days off per year. Studies show people take less time off with unlimited PTO, and we don't want that. We want you rested."

Q: "What's the timeline for profitability?"
A (Michael): "Q3 2026 is our target. We're investing heavily in growth now while market opportunity is huge. But we have clear path to profitability and strong unit economics."

Q: "Any plans for an IPO?"
A (Sarah): "Not in the near term. We're heads down building great products. But we're building the company with that long-term optionality. Ask me again in 2027!"

Q: "How are we differentiating from Google Drive and Dropbox?"
A (Amanda): "Three ways: (1) Zero-knowledge encryption - we literally can't see your data, (2) Enterprise compliance out of the box, (3) AI-first features that actually save time. We're targeting security-conscious businesses, not consumers."

Q: "What's the engineering career ladder look like?"
A (Raj): "We're publishing our career ladder framework in February. Clear IC and management tracks up to Staff/Principal and Engineering Director levels. Growth is important to us."

Q: "How can we get more involved in open source?"
A (Raj): "Great question! We're launching an open source contribution policy next month. You'll get 4 hours per month to contribute to projects you care about. Plus, we're open-sourcing some of our internal tools in Q2."

Q: "What happened with the December 15th outage?"
A (Raj): "DNS configuration issue caused 1.5 hour outage. We've implemented redundant DNS providers and improved our runbooks. Full postmortem is on our status page. Won't happen again."

Q: "Any remote work policy changes coming?"
A (Sarah): "No changes. We're staying remote-first. Quarterly meetups will continue. We might add optional co-working stipend to more roles based on feedback."

Q: "When will we get SOC 2 Type II for the new London data center?"
A (Raj): "June 2025. We're in the observation period now. On track for certification."

Q: "Are bonuses affected by company performance or just individual?"
A (Michael): "It's 70% individual performance, 30% company performance. Since we exceeded our goals, everyone benefits. Details in your bonus letter."
